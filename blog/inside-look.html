Market Preview

Inside Look

April 2026

This is our first rodeo!

There's something special about a first. The first time a space fills with people, music, and energy in a new way. That's exactly what's coming to Duncan Park.

This isn't just about shopping local. It's about creating a space where people can slow down, spend time outside, and enjoy being part of the community.

Throughout the day, you'll hear live music playing in the background while you wander through the market. Kids can stop by for face painting, adding a little extra fun and color to the day. Around the park, a lineup of food trucks will be serving up everything from quick bites to full meals, making it easy to grab something and stay awhile.

You'll also find all of the vendors from our Vendor Roll Call, bringing fresh produce, local honey, baked goods, dairy, and handmade items. It's a chance to meet the people behind the products and support small, local businesses in a meaningful way.

And of course, there's plenty of room to just hang out. Duncan Park's open layout makes it easy to spread out a blanket, enjoy a picnic, and take in the atmosphere. For families, there will even be a small tot basketball game, giving kids a chance to jump in and be part of the action.

At its core, this market is about more than what you can buy. It's about creating a place where people come together, whether that's for music, food, or simply a good morning in the park.

We hope you'll come be part of the first one.
