Who can you expect to see at our farmer's market?

One of the best parts of any farmers market is getting to know the people behind the goods. Here's a look at a few of the local vendors you'll find at the market.

Sunny Ridge Produce

A small family-run farm bringing fresh, seasonal vegetables straight from the field to your table. Known for their tomatoes, leafy greens, and whatever's just been harvested that week.

Golden Prairie Honey Co.

Local beekeepers producing raw, unfiltered honey with rich flavor that reflects the surrounding landscape. They also offer beeswax candles and small-batch infused honey varieties.

Willow Creek Dairy

Specializing in fresh milk, cream, and handcrafted cheeses, all made with care from pasture-raised cows. Their rotating selection of soft cheeses is always a crowd favorite.

Red Barn Bakeshop

A homegrown bakery serving up fresh breads, pastries, and seasonal treats made from scratch. Everything is baked in small batches, so getting there early is never a bad idea.
